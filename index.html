WHO AM I?

Hi, it's nice to meet you! My name is Isabella Quiros. I am 19 years old and I am a student at the University of Waterloo in the Global Business and Digital Arts program. 

WHERE AM I FROM?

Even though I live in Waterloo right now, my hometown is Newmarket, Ontario. That is where I spent the majority of my life up until I went off to university.

In terms of my ethnicity, I come from a Colombian background. Both of my parents were born and raised in Colombia, so I was raised under hispanic culture and tradition and I can also speak Spanish!

WHAT ARE MY SKILLS?

I am a very easy going and approachable person. In my day-to-day life, I like meeting new people and starting random conversations. I love having an active social life, making new friends, and keeping my days busy.

In a more professional environment, I like working in teams and hearing new ideas from different people with different perspectives.

Personally, I have a very firm work ethic. I am often disciplined regardless of motivation, and I am consistent, hard-working, and responsible. I usually excel in the visual, organizational, and creative components of the tasks I am given, and I always pay close attention to detail, which makes me a bit of a perfectionist.

WHAT DO I LIKE TO DO?

As for my personal hobbies, I really enjoy listening to music. From the moment I wake up to the moment I fall asleep, I almost always have a song playing in my ears. My favourite genres are old-school rap, rock, metal, classical, R&B, and jazz/blues.

I have also recently picked up the electric guitar as another hobby; I have no music theory knowledge, but I use the internet as a resource to help me learn chords or specific song riffs.

I also enjoy weightlifting; I have been going to the gym at least four times a week for a few years now. Fitness is a crucial part of my daily routine that I wouldn’t be able to function without!
